About the Project

This AI-Based Glioma Prognosis System allows for non-invasive, accurate, and explainable risk stratification of brain tumors.

Technical Architecture

  • Backend: Python, Flask, SQLite
  • Machine Learning: Scikit-learn (Voting Classifier: LR, RF, SVM, XGBoost)
  • Deep Learning: TensorFlow/Keras (CNN for MRI analysis)
  • Explainability (XAI): SHAP (Global/Local importance), LIME

Clinical Relevance

Gliomas are the most common primary brain tumors. Accurate prognosis is critical for treatment planning. This system integrates multiple data modalities to provide a comprehensive risk assessment score, aiding clinicians in decision-making.
